
Set proxy for maven:

	go to /usr/share/maven/conf/settings.xml

	add 
<proxy>
	<active>true</active>
	<protocol>http</protocol>
	<username>proxyuser</username>
	<password>proxypass</password>
	<host>proxy.iiit.ac.in</host>
	<port>8080</port>
	<nonProxyHosts>localhost|*.iiit.ac.in</nonProxyHosts>
</proxy>
